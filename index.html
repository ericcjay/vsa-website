Mission Statement

The Vietnamese Student Association (VSA) is a student-run organization that seeks to promote the Vietnamese culture and provide a supportive community for students at CSUS.

We hold regular events, collaborate with other student organizations, and offer a space for students to connect with others who share a passion for Vietnamese culture.
